Pricing FAQ

Who handles the payment?

All subscription payments are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited, acting as our Merchant of Record. Paddle handles card processing, global tax (VAT/GST) calculation and remittance, invoicing, and chargebacks. We never see or store your payment card details. See our Privacy Policy for more on how we protect your data.

Is tax included in the listed price?

Prices are shown in US dollars before tax. Paddle will automatically calculate and add the applicable local VAT, GST, or sales tax at checkout based on your billing country. You'll see the final total before you confirm.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel any time from the Paddle-hosted customer portal (link in your order confirmation email) or by emailing alpit@exafabs.ai. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep access until then, and your account automatically returns to the Free tier.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes — every first-time buyer is covered by our 14-day no-questions-asked refund window. Full details in our Refund Policy.

Can I try the paid tiers before buying?

The Free tier of every product is not a time-limited trial — it's genuinely free forever. Use it for as long as you like, and only upgrade when you need the Pro features. Combined with our 14-day refund window, this effectively gives you a risk-free evaluation of any Pro tier.

Do you offer discounts for students, non-profits, or academic researchers?

Yes. Email alpit@exafabs.ai from your institutional email with a short note about how you'll use the product, and we'll arrange a significant discount or free Pro access on a case-by-case basis.

Can I pay annually and save?

Yes. Annual billing saves roughly 25–35% versus monthly on every product that offers a Pro tier. Select the annual option at checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

Paddle accepts all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and SEPA Direct Debit in supported countries. For annual Team/Enterprise plans, wire transfer is available on request.

Is FABS & CHIP Tracker Pro really launching Q2 2026?

The Free tier of FABS & CHIP Tracker launches first, with all core features unlocked. Paid Pro tiers go live once our billing integration with Paddle is fully provisioned. In the meantime, every feature currently marked "Pro" is usable on the Free tier — there's nothing stopping you from starting today.
