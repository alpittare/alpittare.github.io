Delete Your Account

FastAI Health Coach by EXAFABS

We're sorry to see you go. You can delete your FastAI Health Coach account and all associated data using either of the methods below.

Method 1 (Recommended): Delete From Within the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly inside the FastAI Health Coach app:

  1. Open the FastAI Health Coach app on your phone
  2. Tap Profile (bottom-right tab)
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account
  4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

Your account and all associated data are deleted immediately upon confirmation. This is the method required by Apple App Store Guideline 5.1.1(v) and works identically on iOS and Android.

Method 2: Email Request

If you've already uninstalled the app, send us an email and we'll process the deletion within one business day:

Request Account Deletion

Or email us directly at: alpit@exafabs.ai with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

What Gets Deleted

When your deletion is processed (immediately for in-app deletion, within one business day for email requests), the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account profile and email address
  • All meal logs and food entries
  • Fasting schedules and history
  • Health goals and preferences
  • AI coaching conversation history
  • Any uploaded meal photos
  • Subscription and purchase records on our side (the App Store / Play Store manages billing separately — see below)
Important — Cancel Your Subscription First: Deleting your FastAI account does not automatically cancel an active subscription. Cancel before deleting your account using:
  • iOS: Settings app → Apple ID (your name at top) → Subscriptions → FastAI Health Coach → Cancel Subscription
  • Android: Google Play Store → Profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → FastAI Health Coach → Cancel

Data Retention

Account data is removed immediately upon in-app deletion or within one business day for email requests. Some anonymized, aggregated data (e.g., daily active user counts that cannot be linked back to you) may be retained for service operation. Backups containing your data are purged within 30 days of deletion.
