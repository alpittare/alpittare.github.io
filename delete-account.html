We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your FastAI Health Coach account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, send an email to our support team:

Request Account Deletion

Or email us directly at: privacy@exafabs.ai with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove the following data within 30 days:

  • Your account profile and email address
  • All meal logs and food entries
  • Fasting schedules and history
  • Health goals and preferences
  • AI coaching conversation history
  • Any uploaded meal photos
  • Subscription and purchase records (note: Google Play manages billing separately)
Important: If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through Google Play before requesting account deletion. Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. To cancel, go to Google Play Store → Subscriptions → FastAI Health Coach → Cancel.

Data Retention

After your deletion request is processed, all personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days. Some anonymized, aggregated data (such as usage statistics) may be retained for service improvement purposes but cannot be linked back to you.
