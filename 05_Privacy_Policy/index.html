Privacy Policy

CrickBot AI GoalBot AI BaseHit AI Effective Date: March 28, 2026

This privacy policy explains how CrickBot AI, GoalBot AI, and BaseHit AI ("the Games"), developed by Alpit Tare under GameHub Studios ("Developer", "we", "us"), handle your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

The Games do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All game data is stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage (localStorage) or the native app's local storage.

Data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Game progress (scores, levels, achievements, unlocked items)
  • In-game currency balance (coins earned or purchased)
  • User preferences (audio settings, difficulty, equipped items)
  • Gameplay statistics (matches played, win/loss records)

2. Data Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate any servers or databases that receive your game data. No information leaves your device at any time during normal gameplay.

If you uninstall the app or clear your browser data, all locally stored game data will be permanently deleted.

3. In-App Purchases

The Games offer optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect, process, or store any payment information. All financial transactions are handled by Apple in accordance with their privacy policies.

Purchase receipts are validated through Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not have access to your payment method, billing address, or other financial details.

4. Third-Party Services

The Games do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. No SDKs that collect user data are included in the Games.

5. Children's Privacy

The Games do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since no personal data is collected from any user, the Games comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no data to share.

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete all game data by:

  • Uninstalling the app from your device
  • Clearing the app's storage in your device settings
  • Clearing your browser's localStorage (for web versions)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Games after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
