Privacy Policy

How EXAFABS.AI collects, uses, and protects your information across our apps and games.

📱 Covered Applications

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications developed and published by EXAFABS.AI, including:

CrickBot AI
GoalBot AI
BaseHit AI
Survival Arena AI
Infinite Voyager AI

This policy also covers the EXAFABS.AI website at exafabs.ai and any future applications we publish.

🔍 Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact information (name, email) when you reach out via our contact form or support email
  • Feedback, bug reports, or feature requests you submit

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device model, operating system version, screen resolution)
  • App usage data (session duration, features used, in-game progress)
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics to improve app stability
  • General location data (country/region level only, not precise GPS)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise GPS location data
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, or microphone
  • We do not collect financial or payment card information directly — all purchases are handled securely by Apple App Store or Google Play
  • We do not collect data from children under 13 (see Children's Privacy section below)

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our apps and games
  • To fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • To understand how players interact with our games and improve the experience
  • To respond to your support requests and feedback
  • To send important updates about our apps (only if you opt in)
  • To comply with legal obligations

🔗 Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play — for app distribution and in-app purchases
  • Apple GameKit / Game Center — for leaderboards and achievements
  • Analytics services — for anonymous usage statistics and crash reporting
  • Ad networks — some free versions may display ads; ad partners may collect device identifiers for ad personalization (you can opt out via your device settings)

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

🛡️ Data Storage & Security

  • Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device
  • Any data transmitted to our servers uses industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL)
  • We retain collected data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy
  • We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information

While no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, we take commercially reasonable steps to protect your personal information.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at alpit@exafabs.ai and we will promptly delete that information.

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

✅ Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data
  • Request a portable copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at alpit@exafabs.ai.

💳 In-App Purchases

Some of our games offer optional in-app purchases (coins, power-ups, cosmetic items). All transactions are processed entirely through Apple App Store or Google Play. EXAFABS.AI does not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card numbers, or billing details.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the bottom of this page and, where appropriate, notify you through our apps or website. Your continued use of our apps after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
