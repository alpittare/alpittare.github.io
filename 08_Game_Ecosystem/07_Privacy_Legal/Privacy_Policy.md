# Privacy Policy

**CrickBot AI | GoalBot AI | BaseHit AI**

**Effective Date:** March 30, 2026

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how our sports simulation games (CrickBot AI, GoalBot AI, and BaseHit AI) collect, use, and protect your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining transparency about our data practices.

**Key Principle:** We collect minimal data necessary to deliver gameplay and leaderboard features. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII).

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Locally Stored Data
- **Game State:** Player progress, settings, game configurations stored locally on your device
  - Approximate size: <20 KB per game
  - Storage: Device localStorage
  - Persistence: Retained until app deletion
- **Game Sessions:** In-game statistics, score history, playtime records

### 2.2 Server-Side Data
- **Anonymous Player ID:** Unique identifier generated per device (no PII attached)
- **Game Session Data:** Match results, scores, timestamps
- **Purchase History:** Record of in-app purchases for receipt validation and refund tracking
- **Leaderboard Data:** Nickname, score, rank (to support competitive multiplayer features)

### 2.3 What We Do NOT Collect
- Email addresses (not required)
- Real names or personal identification
- Location data (GPS, IP geolocation tracking)
- Device identifiers beyond anonymous player ID
- Browsing history or external app usage
- Phone number or address
- Health/fitness data (despite sports theme)

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

### 3.1 Convex (Backend Infrastructure)
- **Purpose:** Game server, database, real-time leaderboard synchronization
- **Data Shared:** Anonymous player ID, game session data, scores
- **Privacy:** [Convex Privacy Policy](https://www.convex.dev/privacy)
- **Data Location:** Convex-managed servers

### 3.2 Superwall (In-App Payments)
- **Purpose:** VIP subscription management, virtual currency transactions
- **Data Shared:** Purchase history, subscription status, transaction records
- **Privacy:** [Superwall Privacy Policy](https://www.superwall.com/privacy)
- **Note:** Payment processing handled by Superwall; we do not store payment card details

### 3.3 Apple (App Store & iOS)
- **Purpose:** App distribution, in-app purchase processing, analytics
- **Data Shared:** App install data, crash reports (if enabled), anonymous usage metrics
- **Privacy:** [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/)
- **IDFA:** We do not request IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers)

### 3.4 Sentry (Optional - Error Tracking)
- **Purpose:** Production error monitoring and crash reporting
- **Data Shared:** Stack traces, error context (non-personally identifiable)
- **Privacy:** [Sentry Privacy Policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/)
- **Opt-Out:** Disable in app settings if preferred

---

## 4. Data Retention

### 4.1 Local Data
- **Duration:** Persists on device indefinitely until app uninstallation
- **Backup:** Backed up with device iCloud/Android backup if enabled by user
- **Deletion:** Automatically deleted upon app removal

### 4.2 Server Data
- **Game Sessions:** Retained for 2 years for leaderboard integrity and historical analytics
- **Anonymous Player ID:** Retained indefinitely as unique identifier
- **Purchase History:** Retained for 7 years per financial audit requirements
- **Inactive Accounts:** Accounts with no activity for 5+ years may be archived

### 4.3 User Deletion Rights (GDPR)
- Users may request deletion of their anonymous player ID and associated server data
- Submit deletion requests to alpit@exafabs.ai with player ID
- Response within 30 days
- Local device data must be deleted by user (uninstall app)

## 5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

**We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).**

- **Age Restriction:** Services are not directed to children under 13
- **No Collection from Minors:** We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13
- **Parental Consent:** If a parent/guardian discovers we have collected data from a child under 13 without consent, contact alpit@exafabs.ai immediately for deletion
- **No Behavioral Tracking:** No targeting, profiling, or behavioral advertising
- **Minimal Data:** No persistent IDs tied to children; game sessions cannot be individually tracked to identifiable persons

## 6. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

### 6.1 Your Rights
- **Right to Access:** Request copy of your data
- **Right to Deletion:** Request deletion of your anonymous player ID and associated records
- **Right to Data Portability:** Request your game data in portable format
- **Right to Restriction:** Request we limit data processing
- **Right to Object:** Opt-out of analytics or optional services

### 6.2 How to Exercise Your Rights
Submit requests to alpit@exafabs.ai with:
- Player ID
- Clear description of your request
- Copy of government ID for verification

We respond within 30 days.

### 6.3 Legal Basis for Processing
- **Performance of Service:** Game state, leaderboard data
- **Legitimate Interest:** Analytics, game improvement
- **Compliance with Legal Obligations:** Purchase records (tax/fraud prevention)

## 7. Cookies & Tracking Technology

**We do NOT use cookies.** Our apps exclusively use localStorage for client-side persistence.

- **localStorage:** Non-expiring storage on your device for game progress
- **Session Storage:** Temporary data cleared when app closes
- **No Third-Party Tracking:** We do not enable third-party cookie or pixel tracking
- **No Advertising IDs:** We do not request or use IDFA, AAID, or equivalent

---

## 8. Data Security

### 8.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
- **Server Security:** Convex infrastructure includes encryption at rest
- **API Validation:** Server-side validation of all requests to prevent injection attacks
- **Rate Limiting:** Protection against DDoS and brute-force attacks

### 8.2 Limitations
We cannot guarantee absolute security. Transmission of data via internet is inherently at risk. Users are responsible for device security (lock screen, biometric protection).

### 8.3 Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach affecting user data, we will notify affected users within 30 days via in-app notification or email (if collected).
